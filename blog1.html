How Parcel Started

Parcel didn’t begin as a polished idea — it started as a question: why does hardware feel so isolating? Why do people build alone, when the joy comes from sharing?

We imagined something different. A system where building wasn’t the end, but the beginning of exchange. Where every project carried a story, and every shipment created a connection.

That’s how Parcel was born — a loop of making, shipping, and receiving. Not just products, but pieces of people.
