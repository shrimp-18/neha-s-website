About Me

Hey there!

I'm a 17 year old girl born in Dehradun,India. I got my primary,middle and high school education from Ann Mary School,Dehradun. I'd call myself a flâneur (/flaˈnəː/ noun: a person who saunters around observing society.) Since childhood i have felt emotions much more deeply than others. I often get lost in moments! Out of my infine hobbies, one of the things i like to do the most is people watching; it brings me an odd joy which nothing else does. I've also been told that i have insane memory; I remember moments to the tiniest detail event after years have passed. I almost never forget people whom i liked even for a tiny moment. I believe in fate and manifestation; you'd often find me praying at 11:11 and getting super excited when coincidences happen. I fall in love with people, places and things easily. For me love has never been a restrictive concept; instead i believe our sole purpose in life is to love as much as we can, deeply ! I've loved many people and things in life and i hope to love more !
