projects.

GameBit

GameBit is a DIY portable gaming console inspired by the Game Boy Micro. It has a 2inch~ LCD screen, 8 tactile functionality buttons, 2 Power buttons and sound capabilities. It uses a Raspberry Pi02W for all functionality and is powered with a Raspberry Pi battery. The PCB(HAT) is enclosed in a cute case.

Hacker Card

HackerCard is a NFC based business card.It instantly shares digital info (contacts, social media, website) by tapping it with an NFC-enabled smartphone, simplifying networking. It works without a battery and harvests energy from the phone to light up the LED.

ESSync

ESSync (Electronic Scoring System- Sync) is a gesture-controlled referee wristband that automates match timing in Taekwondo. It uses an accelerometer to detect sudden hand movements used by traditional referees to start and stop the match effectively without losing seconds.

Clover

Clover is an Artificial Intelligence powered health robot. It can measure Vitals(heart rate, body temperature) and environmental conditions like AQI, Temperature, Humidity, Harmful gases and pollutants and display them on an LCD screen.

Zephy

Zephy is an Artificial Intelligence powered weather station. It measures environmental conditions like AQI, Temperature, Humidity, Harmful gases and Pollutants, Rainfall and Earthquakes and displays them on an LCD Screen.

Drone

A remote-controlled drone built using an Arduino Uno, featuring an onboard camera for real-time aerial imaging. FlySky-i6X controller is used and components like accelerometer, transmitter/reciever, motors, and Electronic Stability Controllers
